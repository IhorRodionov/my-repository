I have many different hobbies, but such of them keep apart.

Diving

I have been doing it for 4 years and quite professionally. On my account there are already more than a hundred dives,
including technical ones. I love the aquatic environment as it gives a feeling of freedom and independence from the surroundings.
It gives you a break from the surrounding thoughts and re-comprehend some of your actions and thoughts.

Working with a firearm

This type of weapon gives a sense of permissiveness and strength. You need to be able to overcome this, since this can be
extremely dangerous in any situation. I love weapons for their reliability and some grace and beauty. It helps to gather yourself
into a fist and continue to work further and makes you think about the consequences of any of your decisions.

Aikido

The philosophy of the East makes you think about the eternity of the world and about your soul. None of us are imperfect and then you realize it.
Improving the mind and body is the true path of a warrior in aikido, and this is the only thing that matters.
